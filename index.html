research

For my Ph.D., I documented the origins, distribution, and fate of the methane isotopologues 12CH4, 13CH4, 12CH3D, and 13CH3D in nature. More broadly, I have interests in the elements of life (carbon, hydrogen, sulfur, ...), their chemistry in surface and subsurface environments, and what they reveal about the movement of aqueous and organic fluids in the Earth's crust.
