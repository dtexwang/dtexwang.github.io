About

This is my (former) academic homepage.

If you are looking for code or data, it might be on my Github. Otherwise, contact me if you are looking for something that is not here.

I was a Ph.D. student with Shuhei Ono at MIT, and before that, an undergrad tech for Andre Ellis at Cal State Los Angeles. For my Ph.D., I built instruments and models to study how methane and other reduced gases coming from muds, mammals, and mantle rocks were produced. I also went to sea a few times to study the ocean carbon cycle[1] and life beneath the seafloor[2].

Now, I am a research geologist with ExxonMobil (NYSE:XOM) in Houston, Texas.
